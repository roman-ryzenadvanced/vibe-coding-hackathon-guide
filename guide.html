06 // PRO TIPS

Winning Strategies

Show Your Research
Judges love data-backed decisions. Use --emit=html to generate a shareable HTML brief showing exactly what Reddit, X, and HN users are saying about the problem you solve. Real quotes with engagement metrics beat slides every time.
Use Comparison Mode
Run /last30days "Your App vs Competitor A vs Competitor B" to produce a head-to-head comparison table. This shows judges you understand the landscape and have a genuine gap to exploit.
Watchlist for Live Demos
Set up watchlist.py before the hackathon so you can show live trend monitoring during your demo. "This alert came in 2 hours ago: sentiment on X shifted 15% positive about AI code editors." Real-time data in a demo is unforgettable.
